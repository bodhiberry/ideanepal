Empowering Lives, Restoring Dignity

Association for IDEA (Integration, Dignity, Economic Advancement) Nepal is a national level not for profit making non-government organization operated by the people who have experienced Hansen’s disease (Leprosy) for the people with the same experience to support them to restore their dignified life.

Since the organization is founded by the people affected to the people affected, the organization has been tirelessly working to reduce the discrimination and stigma related to leprosy through various projects.

Advocacy for dignified life, psychosocial care for good mental health supports for the perfect well-being and sound livelihood for the people affected by leprosy are the major activities conducted by the organization.
