Our Partners

Working Together for a Better Future

Nepra e. V.

Nepra e.V. collaborated with IDEA Nepal for the first time to implement emergency COVID-19 relief efforts. The reporting was timely, detailed, and professional, which has encouraged us to continue implementing further projects in partnership with IDEA Nepal..

Sasakawa Leprosy (Hansen’s Disease) Initiative

The Sasakawa Leprosy (Hansen’s Disease) Initiative is a strategic alliance that links together the WHO Goodwill Ambassador for Leprosy Elimination, Sasakawa Health Foundation, and The Nippon Foundation for the purpose of achieving a leprosy-free world.
