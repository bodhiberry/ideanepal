Our Projects

Project List – Association for IDEA Nepal

1. Advocacy & Awareness Project

  • Promoting dignity and human rights for people affected by leprosy
  • Reducing stigma and discrimination in communities
  • Policy advocacy and social inclusion initiatives

2. Psychosocial Support & Mental Health Project

  • Counseling and emotional support services
  • Community-based mental health programs
  • Well-being and confidence-building activities

3. Livelihood & Economic Empowerment Project

  • Skill development and income-generating activities
  • Financial support (seed money for small businesses)
  • Promoting sustainable livelihoods

4. Education Support Project

  • Formal education support for affected communities
  • Scholarships for students affected by leprosy
  • Awareness programs to promote education access

5. Recovery & Rehabilitation Project

  • Construction of permanent shelters
  • Home renovation and maintenance support
  • Sanitation facilities (toilet construction)
  • Implemented in Province No. 1 (Biratnagar & Sunsari)
  • Supported 5 families

6. Emergency Support Project (COVID-19 Response)

  • “COVID-19 Support Project for Persons Affected by Leprosy”
  • Distribution of seed money to beneficiaries
  • Scholarship support to 20 individuals
Supported by Sasakawa Health Foundation, Japan

7. Community Support Project

  • Community engagement and outreach programs
  • Support to vulnerable families
  • Volunteer-led assistance and local coordination

8. Housing Support Project

  • Building houses for affected people
  • Improving living conditions
  • Ensuring safe and dignified shelter
